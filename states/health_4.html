Best Air Purifier For Allergies

Air purifiers having the appropriate filtration mechanism have been shown in multiple studies to help reduce allergens in the air and prevent allergic reactions. Most air purifiers also filter dissolved toxic gases and pollutants to give you fresh, clean air. This can greatly increase your quality of life if you suffer from allergies or asthma.

When deciding on which air purifier to get, there are three main considerations you need to make:

  • Size: Check that the Clean Air Delivery Rate (CADR) is sufficient to purify the space you are getting it for.
  • Features: Air purifiers come with a range of features including options to automate settings, humidifying, cooling, heating as well as collecting data on the air in your home.
  • Style: While size and functionality are important, also consider the design element, to add to the decor in your home.
