Phone Plans of The Year

Finding the right smartphone plan doesn’t have to be complicated or hard. In this article, we’ve analyzed a range of phone plans to help you find the best value. Whether you’re looking for a solo plan, a family plan, or a plan with tons of data, you’ll find what you need here. Let’s get started.

Best Cell Phone Plans: Average User

Boost

-Plan name: Boost Unlimited

-Price (monthly): $50 (includes taxes and fees)

-Plan at a glance: Unlimited texts, minutes and data

T-Mobile

-Plan name: T-Mobile Simply Prepaid 10GB plan

-Price (monthly): $40

-Plan at a glance: 10GB of data + unlimited talk and text

-Why we like it: This prepaid plan’s hotspot access and unlimited music streaming give it great value. You also get 10GB which is more than enough data for most cell phone users.

Best Cell Phone Plans: Unlimited

T-Mobile

-Plan name: T-Mobile Essentials

-Price (monthly): $65; $60 with $5 autopay discount.

-Plan at a glance: Unlimited minutes, text messages, and high-speed data. You also get unlimited 3G mobile hotspot data

-Why we like it: This plan gives users 5G network access and unlimited international texting in more than 210 countries.

Boost

-Plan name: Boost Unlimited

-Price (monthly): $50 (includes taxes and fees)

-Plan at a glance: Unlimited minutes, texts, and data

-Why we like it: This Boost plan has a higher mobile hotspot data speed (12GB) than similarly priced plans offered by its competitors.

Best Cell Phone Plans: Families

Boost

-Plan name: Boost Mobile Unlimited

-Price (monthly, four lines): $140 (includes taxes and fees)

-Plan at a glance: Unlimited text, talk, and data, plus SD video streaming and 12GB of mobile hotspot per line.

-Why we like it: This Boost plan includes unlimited text, talk, data, and partner music streaming. You also get enough high-speed mobile hotspot data at a reasonable price.

T-Mobile

-Plan name: T-Mobile Essentials

-Price (monthly, four lines): $125; $105 with autopay discount

-Plan at a glance: Unlimited data, text messages, and minutes 

-Why we like it: This plan is an affordable, entry-level unlimited plan with a mobile hotspot (at 3G speeds) and unlimited texting from 200+ countries.
